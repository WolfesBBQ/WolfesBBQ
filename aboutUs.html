About Us

Welcome to Wolfes BBQ – A True Taste of Kokomo

Founded in 2023, Wolfes BBQ is more than just a place to eat—it’s a homegrown gathering spot where friends, families, and food lovers come together to enjoy authentic, handcrafted barbecue made the old-fashioned way. Nestled right here in Kokomo, Indiana, Wolfes stands as a proud local favorite built on tradition, flavor, and community.

From the moment you walk through the door, you’ll notice the warmth of a true hometown joint—smiling faces, the smell of hickory smoke drifting from the pit, and a menu crafted with care. Every rib, brisket slice, pulled pork sandwich, and smoked sausage link is prepared low and slow, honoring the time-tested methods that make real barbecue unforgettable.

Whether you’re grabbing lunch with coworkers, celebrating with family, or just craving that perfect bite of smoky, savory goodness, Wolfes BBQ is your place to relax, indulge, and feel at home. We believe great barbecue brings people together—and that’s exactly what we’re here to do.
