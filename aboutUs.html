Welcome to Wolfes BBQ – A True Taste of Kokomo

At Wolfes BBQ, we’re more than a restaurant, we’re a homegrown gathering place where friends, families, and food lovers come together to enjoy authentic barbecue made the old-fashioned way. Located right here in Kokomo, Indiana, Wolfes was founded with a simple but powerful mission: serve high-quality, slow-smoked barbecue with heart, hospitality, and Hoosier pride.
