About Us

Welcome to Smoky Trails BBQ, where we take pride in the art of authentic barbecue. Our pitmasters use hickory and oak to smoke ribs, brisket, and pulled pork for hours until each bite melts in your mouth. Come experience flavors inspired by Southern tradition and served with a side of hospitality.
